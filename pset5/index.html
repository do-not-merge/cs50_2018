The Beginning

Established in 1995, this legend initially grew up in Brazil. Once his father decided that pursuing middle-class ambitions in metropolitan São Paolo was not enough to make a poignant, indie documentary worthy of an Oscar he decided to move to the U.S. with his wife,—this at a time where most Americans he met thought all Brazilians were models who spoke Spanish and played football. He realized only one of those things wasn't true.

Hooked? Yessir.

CEC@UCLA

In the true spirit of Hollywood, I got involved in the entertainment industry through the Campus Events Commission in college.

Think "live entertainment providers for students": free concerts featuring local (and international) artists; celebrity (or niche) speaker panels, and movie studios working with us to show movies to Bruins before they came out.

It was neat leading the Speakers team and #networking with talent managers and their clients. And, true to form, I left all that behind for the engineering industry. I'm quite the wanderer, yes.
